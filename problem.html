Q1the empty seat

A cinema has 100 seats, numbered 1 up to that number. Every seat is sold except one. People walk in one at a time and show their ticket number. You cannot write them all down, so you keep only one running total in your head.

If everyone had come, the ticket numbers would add up to 5050. But one person never showed. Your running total stopped at 4909.

Which seat is empty? Show the subtraction, and explain in one line how you found it without remembering every ticket.

Q2stuck at the finish

10 friends split a huge stack of papers to grade, one pile each. They all grade at exactly the same speed. Most finish in ten minutes. One is still grading an hour later. Nobody is slow, nobody made a mistake.

What must have happened to that one pile? Give one simple way to prevent it, and say in your own words why just adding more friends would not fix it.
